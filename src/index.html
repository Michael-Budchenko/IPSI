FAQs

IPSICell’s Pay as You Go SIM doesn’t require you to sign up for 3, 6, or 12 months or pre-pay for months ahead. You will know exactly how much a single month costs with us, and you pay as you go, no hidden fees or special requirements. Want to go cheaper? Connect your IPSIPay account and start earning credits towards your IPSICell mobile plan.

Yes, you can order a Pay as You Go SIM card by IPSICell without getting an IPSIPay wallet. Still, we recommend opening an IPSIPay account to enjoy more convenient SIM card management, add-ons, and bonuses.

If you connect your IPSICell account to an IPSIPay wallet, the app will automatically pre-fill the necessary data in the form. If you order a SIM card without integrating with IPSIPay, you will need to enter your First & Last name, email address, phone number, shipping, and payment information.

It takes up to 5 days to deliver your card to the shipping address once you confirm the order.

IPSICell SIM cards work with core iOS and Android devices.

IPSI services are interconnected and offer maximum benefits when combined. Integrating IPSICell SIM with the IPSIPay wallet, you get IPSICell bonuses for every transaction. Recharging your mobile balance also becomes much easier.
